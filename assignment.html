Website Topic

Journey through the world with food.
Cooking is one of my many interests; I believe I am a decent chef and enjoy devouring food. Everyone aspires to travel across the world, but many of us are unable to do so owing to financial difficulties. My approach of exploring other cultures and regions around the world is through the various cuisines. This website attempts to demonstrate the differences in culinary methods, food variations, and simple recipes that can be used as inspiration.
I intend to exhibit the world map, show common cuisines from many regions, and provide some facts about the specialities. I will also provide links to YouTube videos and recipes for people like me to follow.
